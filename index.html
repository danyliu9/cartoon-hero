One Punch Man

About Saitama

Saitama is the world's strongest hero.
He is the protagonist in the anime One Punch Man, where he beats every foe with a single punch.

Characteristics

  1. Lost his hair after getting strong.
  2. Is extremely strong.
  3. Could possibly destroy the universe.
  4. Is sometimes referred to as "caped-baldy".

Saitama and his friends

Saitama fights monsters and bad guys with other super powerful hero friends of his. His best friend, Genos, is a cyborg that treats him like his master, and regards Saitama as the strongest hero.

  • Bang, AKA Silver Fang
  • Genos, the Demon Cyborg
